About Me

My name is David Müller I am a Prague based videographer and photographer focused on visual storytelling. I work with individuals, brands, and creatives to produce clean, modern, and emotionally driven visuals. I produce all of my projects from start to finish, handling pre-production, operating cameras, shooting, and lastly handling post-production in Lightroom and DaVinci Resolve.

Work

I produce complete creative commercial projects tailored to individual client needs - from concept and shooting to editing and final delivery. My work is shaped by hands-on experience from professional film productions, including the German projects The Method (Wiedemann & Berg) and City of Blood (Amusement Park). I am also currently working as a production PA at Sirena Film, where I continue to develop my skills within a proffesional production enviroment.
